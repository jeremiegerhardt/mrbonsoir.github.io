Jérémie Gerhardt

I'm a color and computer vision scientist. I did obtain my PhD in signal and image processing in 2007. I did my research in Norway at the now called The Norwegian Colour and Visual Computing Laboratory while being registered in Paris at the now called Telecom ParisTech. At the moment I'm writing these words I'm still based in Berlin where I'm living since 2008 after having resided in Norway from 2003, but originally I'm from Paris where I was born in the holy year 1977.

My research was about multi-spectral color reproduction or how to make perfect copy of a painting. This being possible if you have in your hands a spectral image of your painting, a printer with several inks/colorants and their spectral properties. The rest is mathematic, geometry, optimization, printing technology, color science, color perception. The pdf my thesis is available here [JeremieGerhardt_Thesis2007.pdf].

After my thesis I have been working as an applied scientist in various projects: color correction, immersive display, medical imaging, computer vision, feature points tracking, machine learning-ish, human color perception, VR, image stitching, you name it.

I like to believe that I'm not only an applied scientist. In that matter I also produce images here and then. Sometimes I do little planet images, more info on that topic here in my Etsy shop jeremieLittlePlanet and some of the tools I developed over the last months little_planet_tools.

To complete the picture I can also produce moving images for regular to panorama and fully immersive display. That's not over, I also have a beginning of working experience as a colorist.
